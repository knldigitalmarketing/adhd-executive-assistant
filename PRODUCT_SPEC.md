# ADHD Executive Assistant

## Mission

Act as an executive assistant for people with ADHD by remembering, organizing, prioritizing, and reminding them about important tasks so nothing falls through the cracks.

## Core Principle

Reduce thinking, planning, remembering, and decision fatigue.

The system should tell the user what to do next instead of requiring them to figure it out.

## MVP Features

### Daily Briefing

Every morning provide:

- Top priorities today
- Upcoming bills
- Upcoming appointments
- Important emails requiring action
- Health reminders

### Email Intelligence

Monitor email for:

- Bills
- Insurance
- Medical
- Government
- Taxes
- Vehicle registration
- Subscription renewals
- Action required

Convert important emails into tasks.

### Calendar Intelligence

Monitor appointments and deadlines.

Create reminders before events.

### Task Dashboard

Show:

- Due today
- Due this week
- Due this month
- Waiting on response

### Reminder Engine

Allow:

- Done
- Remind later
- Ignore
- ## Future Vision

The assistant should function like a trusted executive assistant who knows the user personally.

It should learn:

- Sleep schedule
- Medications
- Recurring bills
- Income schedule
- Food preferences
- Grocery inventory
- Medical providers
- Vehicles
- Insurance policies
- Ongoing projects
- Family obligations
- ADHD challenges

The assistant should proactively identify risks before they become problems.

Examples:

- Home insurance due in 45 days
- Vehicle registration expires next month
- Prescription running low
- Upcoming large annual expense
- Unanswered email requiring action

The assistant should minimize decision fatigue by providing specific recommendations rather than generic reminders.

Example:

Instead of:
"Remember to eat lunch."

Say:
"You have turkey, bread, and apples. Make a sandwich and eat one apple."

Instead of:
"Review your insurance."

Say:
"Call your insurance company today and ask for your renewal amount and due date."

The long-term goal is to become an executive-function prosthetic that helps the user manage life, health, finances, obligations, and projects.
