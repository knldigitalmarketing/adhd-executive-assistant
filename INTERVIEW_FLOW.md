# Interview Flow

## Interview 1 - Life Snapshot (Required)

Goal: Understand how the user lives.

### Home

* Own Home
* Rent
* Live with Family
* Other

### Work

* Employee
* Self Employed
* Business Owner
* Retired
* Unemployed

### Transportation

* Car
* Truck
* Motorcycle
* Bicycle
* E-Bike
* Bus
* Uber/Lyft
* Walking
* Other

### Health

* Medications to Track
* Medical Appointments to Track

### Family

* Partner
* Children
* Dependents
* Pets

### Finances

* Bills
* Insurance
* Debt
* Investments

### Projects

* Personal Projects
* Business Projects

Result:

Create profiles for each selected area.

---

## Interview 2 - Complete Your Profiles

Only ask questions for profiles identified in Interview 1.

Examples:

### Vehicle Profile

* Year
* Make
* Model
* Mileage

### Home Profile

* Insurance
* Property Taxes
* HOA
* Mortgage

### Business Profile

* LLC
* Domains
* Hosting
* Software Subscriptions

### Pet Profile

* Vet
* Medications
* Food

---

## Interview 3 - Obligation Discovery

Show intelligent checklists based on user profiles.

The user should recognize obligations rather than remember them.

Example:

Business Owner Checklist

* Domains
* Hosting
* Google Workspace
* Accounting Software
* Business Insurance
* Annual Filings

---

## Interview 4 - Continuous Learning

The assistant should continue learning through:

* Email
* Calendar
* User actions
* Shopping activity
* Bills
* Reminders

The interview never truly ends.
