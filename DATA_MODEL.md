# Data Model

## User

The person using the assistant.

## Responsibility Area

Major areas of life.

Examples:

* Home
* Transportation
* Health
* Family
* Pets
* Work
* Business
* Finances
* Projects

## Profile

Detailed information about a responsibility area.

Examples:

* Home Profile
* Motorcycle Profile
* Business Profile
* Health Profile

## Asset

A thing that requires management.

Examples:

* House
* Vehicle
* Motorcycle
* Boat
* LLC
* Domain
* Pet

## Obligation

Something that must be maintained, renewed, monitored, or paid.

Examples:

* Insurance
* Registration
* Property Tax
* License Renewal
* Subscription
* Medication Refill

## Routine

Recurring activities.

Examples:

* Morning Medications
* Breakfast
* Exercise
* Evening Medications
* Bedtime

## Action

A task requiring user action.

Examples:

* Call Insurance Company
* Renew Registration
* Schedule Appointment

## Discovery

Something the assistant finds automatically.

Examples:

* Important Email
* Better Insurance Quote
* Tire Replacement Needed
* Upcoming Renewal

## Timeline Event

An item displayed in the user's daily schedule.

Examples:

* Breakfast
* Appointment
* Medication Reminder
* Project Time Block
* Transition Reminder

## Transition Reminder

A special event designed to help the user stop, switch, or begin activities.

Examples:

* Wrap Up Current Task
* Leave For Appointment
* Switch Projects
* Begin Bedtime Routine
